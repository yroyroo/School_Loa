Computer Science

The Computer Science program at Lyceum of Alabang offers students a comprehensive education in the field of computer science and technology. This program covers a wide range of topics, including programming, algorithms, data structures, software engineering, and computer hardware.

Students in the Computer Science program have the opportunity to develop practical coding skills, work on real-world software projects, and gain a deep understanding of the theoretical foundations of computing. Our dedicated faculty members bring a wealth of experience to the classroom, ensuring that students receive a high-quality education.

Graduates from the Computer Science program are well-prepared for careers in software development, IT consulting, data analysis, and more. Whether you aspire to work in technology companies, startups, or research institutions, our program equips you with the skills and knowledge needed for success in the dynamic field of computer science.
