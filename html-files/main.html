Welcome to Lyceum of Alabang

Where your dreams come true

1.School of Arts

The School of Arts at Lyceum of Alabang is dedicated to fostering creativity, critical thinking, and artistic expression. Our programs offer a wide range of disciplines, including visual arts, performing arts, literature, and humanities. Our faculty members are accomplished artists and scholars who provide students with a rich and engaging learning experience. Students in the School of Arts have the opportunity to explore their creative potential, participate in exhibitions and performances, and gain a deep understanding of the cultural and artistic heritage.

Requirements for Admission:
  • High School Diploma or Equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 2.5
  • Letter of Intent
  • Interview (if required)
  • English Proficiency Test (for non-native English speakers)

2. School of Science

The School of Science at Lyceum of Alabang is committed to excellence in science education and research. Our programs cover a wide spectrum of scientific disciplines, including biology, chemistry, physics, and mathematics. Our state-of-the-art laboratories, dedicated faculty, and research opportunities enable students to develop a strong foundation in scientific principles and conduct cutting-edge research. Graduates from the School of Science are well-prepared for careers in scientific research, healthcare, and technology.

Requirements for Admission:
  • High School Diploma or Equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • High School Transcripts (with a strong focus on science and mathematics courses)
  • Letters of Recommendation (2)
  • Personal Statement (explaining your interest in science)
  • SAT or ACT Scores (optional but recommended)

3. School of Commerce

The School of Commerce at Lyceum of Alabang is dedicated to preparing students for careers in business, finance, and entrepreneurship. Our programs offer a comprehensive understanding of business principles, economics, and management practices. With a strong emphasis on real-world applications, our faculty members bring a wealth of industry experience to the classroom. Students in the School of Commerce gain practical skills, engage in internships, and have access to a network of industry professionals, setting them on a path to success in the world of commerce.

Requirements for Admission:
  • High School Diploma or Equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 2.8
  • High School Transcripts (with a focus on relevant business and mathematics courses)
  • Letters of Recommendation (2)
  • Personal Statement (detailing your interest in commerce and career goals)
  • Interview (if required)
  • SAT or ACT Scores (optional but recommended)
  • Math Proficiency Test (for students without a strong math background)
