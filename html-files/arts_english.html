English

The English program at Lyceum of Alabang is designed to enhance students' language skills, critical thinking, and communication abilities. Through the study of literature, writing, and linguistics, students explore the power of language and its impact on society.

Our experienced faculty members guide students in analyzing literary works, writing effectively, and understanding the nuances of language. Graduates from the English program pursue diverse careers in writing, publishing, education, and more.
