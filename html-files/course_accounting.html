Accounting

The Accounting program at Lyceum of Alabang equips students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the field of financial accounting and auditing. Students learn about financial reporting, taxation, and auditing standards, preparing them for careers in finance and accounting.

Our experienced faculty members provide students with a strong foundation in accounting principles and ethical practices. Graduates from the Accounting program pursue careers as certified public accountants (CPAs), financial analysts, and financial advisors.
