Mathematics

The Mathematics program at Lyceum of Alabang offers students a deep dive into the world of mathematics, covering a wide range of mathematical concepts and theories. Students explore topics in algebra, calculus, statistics, and more, gaining a strong foundation in mathematical problem-solving and critical thinking.

Our dedicated faculty members guide students through rigorous coursework, helping them develop analytical skills and mathematical reasoning. Graduates from the Mathematics program are well-equipped for careers in fields such as data analysis, finance, education, and research.
