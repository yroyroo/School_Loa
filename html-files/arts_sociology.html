Sociology

The Sociology program at Lyceum of Alabang delves into the study of human behavior, society, and culture. Students explore topics such as social dynamics, institutions, and the impact of societal changes. This program fosters critical thinking and a deep understanding of the complexities of human interaction.

Our faculty members guide students in examining social issues, conducting research, and analyzing sociological theories. Graduates from the Sociology program are prepared for careers in social work, counseling, research, and advocacy, making a positive impact on communities and society.
